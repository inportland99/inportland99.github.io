Dr. Shah provides consulting and professional development for individual schools and districts. He is passionate about helping teachers gain a deeper understanding of mathematics, getting kids excited about mathematical thinking, and developing thinking skills over memorization in elementary and middle school.

Dr. Shah routinely presents at national, state, and local conferences including National Council of Teachers of Mathematics Annual Conference (2017), National Math Festival (2017), McGraw-Hill Leadership Symposium (2017, 2016, 2015), Mid School Math Conference (2015, 2014), and Ohio Council of Teachers of Mathematics Annual Conference (2017, 2013).

Because of Math Plus' proven results, Dr. Shah is pursued by school districts nationwide to instruct teachers. In Central Ohio, he has provided training to teachers in Westerville, New Albany, Hilliard, Dublin, Worthington, Reynoldsburg, South-Western City and Columbus City Schools.
